Survival Guide

THE 3 COMMANDMENTS OF MOON TRADES

I. Protect The Capital

We do not average down on losing options. If a trade hits our predetermined Stop Loss (usually 10-15%), we cut it immediately. Capital preservation is the only way to survive in this market. Live to trade another day.

II. Secure The Bag Early

When an alert goes up 20% to 30%, we trim a portion of our position and move the Stop Loss to breakeven. From that point on, it is a risk-free trade. We never let a green trade turn red.

III. No Emotional Trading

FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out) kills accounts. If you miss the initial entry callout, you do not chase the premium. You sit on your hands and wait for the next setup. Discipline pays.
